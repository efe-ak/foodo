Layout KIT

Get full control of the layout of your pages and easily create layouts from the most simple - fluid or boxed layouts - to the most advanced layouts with up to 6 sidebars on the same page and specially crafted layouts for complex web apps.

Most simple and common fluid layouts to get started from.

Sidebar Sizes

There are 3 predefined sidebar sizes of 56px (mini menu with icons), a sidebar of 200px (the most common) and a larger 250px sidebar size.
  • Sidebar Size 1 (56px)
  • Sidebar Size 1 (56px) with Reveal
  • Sidebar Size 2 (200px)
  • Sidebar Size 3 (250px)
  • Percentage Sidebar Sizes (25% to 100%)

Fixed Layouts

Fixed or boxed layouts with centered content showcasing most of the sidebar options available with fluid layouts.
  • No Sidebar
  • Overlay Sidebars (Single Toggle Button)
  • Overlay Sidebars (Multiple Toggle Buttons)
  • 2 Inner Sidebars
  • 3 Inner Sidebars
