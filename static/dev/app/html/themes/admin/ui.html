Progress Bars - Colors

Provide up-to-date feedback on the progress of a workflow or action with simple yet flexible progress bars.

    60% Complete

    40% Complete (success)
  • Information:

  • Warning:

  • Inverse:
    40% Complete
  • Default (Gray):
    55% Complete
  • Danger:
    80% Complete
